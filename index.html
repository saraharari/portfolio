APPROACH

Whether you are a pro or novice, telling your story and driving business on the tech speaking circuit can be time-intensive and complicated.

THAT'S WHERE I COME IN.

As a speaking strategist, I help senior tech executives save time.

I identify worthy speaking opportunities, arm you with the details to prepare, and provide you with the market insight to tailor your content and nail your delivery.

I AM AN EXPERIENCED, NEUTRAL, DEDICATED SPEAKING STRATEGIST.

I am not a public speaking coach, a speakers bureau or a PR firm. As a speaking strategist, I have the independence to zero-in on your unique objectives, strengthen your content and design, and showcase your personal brand to audiences around the world.

FOCUS AREAS

MY SPEAKING ENGAGEMENT PHILOSOPHY:

GO GLOBAL: Tap into unfamiliar opportunities abroad - the market-entry and client acquisition prospects are endless.

BE SELECTIVE: Every speaking engagement should be a return on investment of time.

KNOW YOUR OBJECTIVE: Know your end goal and map your path there.

DO BUSINESS: Make sure you’re scheduled for productive conversations, in side meetings, dinners or special events.

STUDY YOUR AUDIENCE: From demographics and culture to topic expertise and business potential.

TEACH: Chances are you know something about your topic that no one else in the audience does.

KEEP IT FRESH: Understand the entirety of the program and ensure you stand out.

SHOW YOUR PERSONAL SIDE: Don’t be afraid to show your personality or personal story on stage.

KEEP IT SHORT: Get your point across while keeping your audience engaged and captivated.

SHARE IT: Record, collect and show off your speaking engagements.

About

Sara Harari is a speaking strategist based in New York. Over the last 15 years, she has worked behind-the-scenes to design programs and speaking engagements for world-class platforms across the US and China including: Clinton Global Initiative, Social Innovation Summit, CES, Global Mobile Internet Conference, the American Chamber of Commerce in Shanghai, YWCA of the City of New York and New York University.

She has worked with partners, speakers and executives across leading brands including: Microsoft, Nike, Comcast NBCUniversal, Target, Intel, Tencent, Twitter, Xiaomi, Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, Patagonia, the North Face, Omidyar Network, Home Depot, HP, The Honest Company, Mars, Lyft, LEGO Group, PwC, eBay, Airbnb, Annenberg Foundation, Vodafone, Golden State Warriors, Harman, Kauffman Foundation, Abbott and Starbucks.

Sara is a contributor to the Huffington Post and is pursuing fluency in Farsi, French, Mandarin and code.
