--- About ---

Sara Harari is a speaking strategist based in New York. Over the last 15 years, Sara has worked behind-the-scenes to design programs and speaking engagements for world-class platforms across the US and China including: Clinton Global Initiative, Social Innovation Summit, CES, Global Mobile Internet Conference, the American Chamber of Commerce in Shanghai, YWCA of the City of New York and New York University.

She has worked with partners, speakers and executives across leading brands including: Microsoft, Nike, Comcast NBCUniversal, Target, Intel, Tencent, Twitter, Xiaomi, Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, Patagonia, the North Face, Omidyar Network, Home Depot, HP, The Honest Company, Mars, Lyft, LEGO Group, PwC, eBay, Airbnb, Annenberg Foundation, Vodafone, Golden State Warriors, Harman, Kauffman Foundation, Abbott and Starbucks.

Sara is a contributor to the Huffington Post and is pursuing fluency in Farsi, French, Mandarin and code.
